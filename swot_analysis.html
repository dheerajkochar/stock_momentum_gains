SWOT analysis of the Nifty 200 momentum study and trading signal

STRENGTHS

High win rate — 77–82%

Validated across 213 trades, 7 years. Not a single-year anomaly.

Simple 3-rule entry

Event return 7–10% + CLV > 0.942 + ATR < 2.0. Computable in 15 min at close.

Positive every year

Exit 1 and Exit 2 never produced a losing year — including COVID (2020) and rate hikes (2022).

Causal mechanism identified

CLV captures institutional supply vacuum at the closing auction — not curve-fitting.

No expensive data required

Entirely from public OHLCV via yfinance. No Bloomberg, no earnings feeds.

Intraday measurement insight

Identified that Day+1 intraday high (+4.12%) vs close (+0.39%) — a finding most research misses.

WEAKNESSES

Very low trade frequency

Only 26 trades/year average. Capital sits idle 355 days. Real return on total capital is ~2–3%.

Gap risk — stops don't protect

-2% stop became -19% in March 2020. Overnight news events bypass stop orders entirely.

Stock concentration risk

JPPOWER, RPOWER, INOXWIND appear repeatedly. Multiple trades in same stock = same bet repeated.

Thin annual samples

2023 had only 6 trades. Year-level statistics are unreliable at this frequency.

Survivorship bias

2025 Nifty 200 list used for entire backtest. Delisted/poor performers may be underrepresented.

No earnings filter

Post-earnings gaps behave differently from price-only breakouts. Mixed signal pool.

OPPORTUNITIES

Expand to Nifty 500

Adding mid and small caps could triple trade frequency from 26/yr to 70–80/yr with same rules.

Add earnings filter

Separating earnings-driven events from price-only breakouts could significantly sharpen edge.

Options strategy overlay

77% directional accuracy makes this a strong candidate for long call or bull call spread overlays to amplify returns.

Scale capital per trade

Same signal works at ₹2L or ₹5L/trade. Annual PnL scales linearly with position size.

Intraday entry refinement

Adding pre-market futures check could avoid catastrophic gap-down stops before they happen.

Apply same framework globally

CLV + ATR methodology is market-agnostic. US, European, or other emerging market equities are natural extensions.

THREATS

Signal decay as adoption grows

If many traders use the same CLV+ATR rules, arbitrage compresses the gap. Edge erodes over time.

2024–2025 gap behaviour change

Gap ≥ 3% at open dropped from 36% (IS) to 0% (2025 OOS). Market structure may have shifted permanently.

Systemic crash events

COVID-style tail events create -15% to -20% overnight gaps that stops cannot protect against.

Regulatory changes

SEBI changes to circuit breakers, auction timing, or F&O rules could alter the closing auction dynamics that CLV depends on.

Algo front-running

Institutional algorithms scanning the same CLV conditions could buy before retail close, compressing the overnight gap opportunity.

Prolonged bear market

Strategy was never tested in a multi-year bear market. 2022 showed strain (₹11,699 vs ₹87,621 in 2020). A full bear cycle is unknown territory.
